WHAT'S IN TABAG'S PLACE?

What is it about Tabag's Place that makes your getaways so memorable? It's the great destination you can call your own, more than the uniqueness, beauty, and better things in life.

RESTAURANT

Chill in the warmth of Tabag's Place while we bring satisfaction to your tummy and heart as we serve you delicious yet very affordable foods.

VIEW DECK

Enjoy the view and take unlimited pictures while overlooking the well-known mountain in our province, Mt. Malindig and Tigwi Sea in our man-made view deck.

FALLS

Appreciate more the beauty of Tabag's Place as you swim in their extra cold "natural falls". After walking down in a steep, hollow and cemented stairway, you'll be able to find this hidden gem.

TABAG'S PLACE GUIDE MAP

Tabag's Restaurant - Located beside the entrance gate of Tabag's Place. Affordable yet delicious foods as well as drinks are served.


Grounds - Wide space and an open area. Sometimes serves as a Camping Ground for tourists who desire to spend the night at Tabag's Place.


Camp Site - Located near the view deck and infront of one of the bungalow house. Most tourists want to occupy this spot during camping because of its view.


View Deck - Man-made view deck and is the main attraction in Tabag's Place because of the overlooking view of Mt. Malindig and Tigwi Sea.


Birdhouse - Home of the pigeons who are in great care of the owners. Located right beside the bungalow.


Tabag Falls - Hidden down the cliff is the Falls and can be reached after walking past down a steep yet hollow stairway.


Tabag Swimming Pool - Located near the falls and is also man-made. This "natural swimming pool" is still under construction.


Mt. Malindig - Considered a potentially active volcano because of the solfataric springs of Malbog. Mt. Malindig is the largest mountain on the province.
