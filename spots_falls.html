Tabag Falls

The falls, hidden deep within Tabag's Place, is becoming a tourist favorite. To get to this, you'll have to trek for 5-10 minutes past a thrilling, steep, and man-made stairway. After that, it's time to unwind at this secluded location. This Tabag Falls, which has an entrance fee of P50 pesos, is a sure-fire tourist attraction because of its clean water that comes from natural springs. Expect Tabag Fallsâ€™ water to be cool because it comes from the mountains that enjoy a moderate to cool temperature.
The swimming area at the foot of the waterfalls is deep. Huge rocks sit around the basin of the Falls. Travelers usually use these rocks as a place to rest and just enjoy the view of the waterfalls. There are also cottages where you can sit and relax that are available for rent, with daily rates ranging from P50 to P100.
The Tabag Swimming Pool, which is still under construction, is located right next to Tabag Falls. This site, which also has running water from natural springs, will undoubtedly make Tabag's Place become a more well-known and popular tourist destination in Marinduque.
