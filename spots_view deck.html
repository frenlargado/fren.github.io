View Deck

Because of its overlooking view of Mt. Malindig, a big potentially active stratovolcano on Buenavista, Marinduque, and the turquoise sea of Tigwi on Torrijos, Marinduque, this man-made view deck is currently the main tourist attraction here in Tabag's Place. The panoramic view of wide lush greenery combination of mountain, trees, fields, and grasses is picturesque enough for one to remember Tabagâ€™s Place.
One can feel the fresh air and cool climate which is no longer experienced in the congested places. You, your family and loved ones can take as many pictures as you like in this corner of Tabag's Place for only P20 pesos. You can also relax here in the mornings while waiting for the sunrise, sipping your hot coffee, and enjoying the natural air that this place provides.
The soft lights that surround the View Deck provide a wonderfully comfortable environment at night. You can still come here in the late afternoon to relax and enjoy the moment while waiting for the sunset. It gets extremely cold in the late afternoon and evening, so bring your jackets if you don't want to catch a cold.
